nathalie

Nathalie is a senior journalist and producer from Los Angeles, expertly tapped into the evolving world of digital multi-media.

She has produced and reported stories from around the world, including a women’s prison in Colombia, and a sacred Māori tattoo ceremony in New Zealand. She uncovered the abuses in elephant tourism in Southeast Asia, trekked to an isolated mountain town on the Georgia/Russia border to report on a local family, and visited Pablo Escobar’s mansion-turned-theme park in Colombia.

Nathalie produces, films, writes, edits, and reports her content.

Before digital journalism, Nathalie was a TV journalist
working for networks like CBS and NBC.

This journey began
because

Nathalie wanted to evolve her craft to tell stories about the world that break down cultural borders.
